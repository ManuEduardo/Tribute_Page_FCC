Here's a time line of Alang Turing life:

  • 1912 - Born in England, London.
  • 1920 - At the age of eight he designed the first laboratory at home.
  • 1931 - Formalize the concept of the turing machine.
  • 1935 - At the age of 22, he was appointed professor at King's College.
  • 1936 - He publishes his study "Computable numbers with an application to ..."
  • 1938 - His dissertation introduced the concept of ordinal logic and the notion of relative computation.
  • 1939 - He begins work at Bletchley Park, a secret army station.
  • 1941 - He begins to decipher the messages of the subamrino U-boar.
  • 1945 - He collaborated in the creation of the Colossus machine.
  • 1948 - First demonstration of the turing machine.
  • 1952 - In an investigation his homosexuality is discovered.
  • 1952 - They condemn him and give him a choice between prison or chemical castration.
  • 1954 - On June 7 he commits suicide by biting an apple sprinkled in cyanide.
  • 1966 - The turing prize recognized as the nobel of computing is created.
  • 1970 - The world finds out what he did in WWII.
  • 2009 - The British Prime Minister apologizes for what happened.
  • 2016 - British government pardons 46,000 gays under "turing law"
